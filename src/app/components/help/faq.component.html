Basic Information

1. What do I need to know to use eXchangily ?

eXchangily is a fully decentralized open-source digital wallet and crypto trading platform. Users are wholly responsible for the security of their digital wallets and crypto assets. The wallet mnemonics and private keys are important to protect your eXchangily wallet. Users control their wallets and assets with their mnemonics and private keys. Never share these with others otherwise you may compromise your wallet security and could lose your digital assets.
As a decentralized wallet and exchange, all transactions will require a gas fee. Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin (BTC) and Ether (ETH), FAB coin, and Dogecoin (DOGE) all have their respective blockchain, called a main coin. To transact these coins, extra coins will need to be allocated for gas as well as main coin for derivative coin transactions on the main chain.
(e.g. If you want to send USDT, you need enough ETH as gas for Ethereum’s blockchain transaction. Likewise, you will need FAB as a gas fee for transactions of EXG.)
NOTE: eXchangily is built on the Kanban blockchain, which takes FAB coin as the primary gas. All transactions and actions on eXchangily will need FAB.

2. Why are there always errors when transferring crypto to eXchangily ?

The exchange needs to confirm the correspondence between the crypto transfer and the eXchangily wallet so it needs time to complete the transaction. (e.g. If you want to transfer BTC and ETH to the exchange, and you transfer BTC first, you will need to wait for the BTC transaction to complete before transferring ETH. It usually takes 30 minutes to 1 hour to complete each transaction.)

3. The crypto left my eXchangily wallet but it is not showing up on the exchange ?

To verify that each transaction is valid and correct, the transaction chain confirms the number of blocks on the original chain before processing the transaction. Please be patient as it usually takes half an hour to one hour to complete the transaction. Sometimes it may take up to two hours. If you do not receive your transaction within two hours, check if there is a button to confirm the deposit. Select the button and verify your wallet password to reconfirm. The exchange should receive the deposit immediately.

4. Why is the exchange alerting that there is not enough ETH when I transfer USDT ?

eXchangily is a decentralized wallet and exchange, so all transactions and actions are carried out based on the chain. USDT is a token on the Ether blockchain so transferring any token on Ether requires a payment of a gas fee to the blockchain. Similarly, transferring EXG and DUSD on the Fast Action Blockchain requires a payment of FAB as the gas fee. You need the corresponding main chain coins in your wallet to transfer cryptocurrencies.

5. I have enough ETH in my wallet. Why did the USDT not transfer to the exchange?

eXchangily uses FAB coin as the gas fee. All transactions or actions on eXchangily require FAB. When depositing cryptos to the exchange or making any buy-or-sell transactions, you need FAB as the gas fee regardless of the coin you trade. If it did not process, check if you have enough FAB.

6. What is adding gas to the exchange? How much do I need to add each time ?

eXchangily takes FAB coin as a gas fee. You need gas before making any transaction or action on the exchange. The amount of gas needed is dependent on the frequency you will trade on eXchangily. Generally a few FAB coins will cover multiple transactions at a time. If you only use the eXchangily for its wallet and not the exchange, you do not really need to add gas.

7. Why did my crypto withdrawal from the exchange not deposit in my wallet ?

The exchange blockchain performs a crypto withdrawal every half hour. The terminal blockchain may become busy and need more time to process. It usually takes 40 minutes to complete a withdrawal, but it could also take several hours or even days due to the third-party chain.

常见问题回答

基本问题

1. 使用亿币需要了解哪些必备的知识 ?

亿币是一个去中心化的开源钱包和交易所系统，用户必须知道，用户对自己的资产安全负有全部责任。具体来说，助记词及由助记词生成的私钥是安全的生命线，拥有助记词或私钥，就完全控制了资产，用户必须妥善安全地保管好它们，不得遗失，不得泄露，否则您将失去全部资产。
另外，用户应该明白，作为去中心化的钱包及交易所，一切操作都需支付燃料费。有些币，如比特币BTC, 以太币ETH, 发币FAB, 莱特币LTC, 狗狗币DOGE等，都有自己独立的区块链，称为主链币，不仅发送主链币需要消耗这些币作为燃料，发送任何主链上的派生币同样需要消耗主链币作为燃料。
例如，如果您想向另一个钱包发送USDT，您的钱包中必须有足够的ETH以支付以太坊网络的燃料费，同理，如果想发送亿币EXG，钱包中需要有足够FAB作为燃料，依此类推 ...
需要特别说明的是，亿币交易所是运行在一个特殊的区块链Kanban上的，Kanban以FAB作为燃料，因此，在交易所中交易任何币中，均仅消耗FAB作为燃料，如果交易所中没有足够的FAB燃料，无法执行任何操作。

2. 为什么在向交易所转币时，经常发生错误?

由于交易所需要确定转账与钱包的对应关系，向交易所转币只能一笔一笔的进行，即当一笔交易完成后，才能再转另一笔，比如您准备向交易所分别存BTC和ETH，你首先提交了一笔BTC存币, 需要等交易所收到这笔后再存另一笔ETH，否则会提示错误。通常完成一笔向交易所转币操作可能需要半个小时至一个小时。

3. 钱包里币已经扣掉了，为什么交易所没收到?

为了确保交易有效无误，交易链通常需要在原链确定了一定的块数后才认为交易有效，所以在存币后请耐心等待，通常需要半个小时至一个小时的时间，有时甚至两个小时，如果更长时间没收到，请检查存币按钮处是否出现了一个确认存币按钮，如果有，点击该按钮并输入钱包密码确认，交易所就立即收到存币了。

4. 为什么转USDT时，提示没有足够ETH?

亿币是去中心化钱包及交易所，一切操作都在链上进行，由于USDT是以太坊区块链上的代币，所以转发任何以太坊上的代币包括USDT都需向以太坊区块链支付燃料费，即使向交易所转币也一样；同理，转发EXG、DUSD等FAB区块链上的币，需要支付FAB燃料费，所以在转代币时，您的钱包里必须有对应的主链币。

5. 钱包里有足够的ETH，为什么向交易所转USDT没收到?

交易所的任何操作都需要在交易所预先充燃料(FAB)，不管向交易所充币还是在交易所执行买卖交易，交易所都必须有足够的燃料，需要特别强调的是，交易所内的一切交易都是以FAB作为燃料的，不管什么币交易。

6. 什么是加燃料，一般要加多少?

加燃料操作是向交易所加FAB，交易所的一切交易都是以FAB作为燃料的，在使用交易所之前，必须首先加燃料，否则，在交易所内什么都不能做，包括向交易所转币，具体需要加多少视您在交易所的交易频率，一般加几个FAB就可以用比较长一段时间。当然，如果您只用钱包，不用交易所，则不需加燃料。

7. 从交易所向钱包提币为什么没收到?

交易所区块链每半小时执行一次提币操作，而目的区块链有时可能比较繁忙，通常提币操作应在40分钟内完成，但因第三方链繁忙的原因，有时需要几个小时甚至更长时间。

8. 我想加燃料，从哪里获得FAB?
